The Future of Saudi Arabia

Mohammed bin Salman

Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia, spearheading the ambitious Vision 2030 plan to revolutionize the Kingdom's economy and modernize society.

Key Initiatives

  • Economic diversification through Vision 2030
  • Progressive social reforms
  • Development of NEOM, a futuristic mega-city
  • Expansion of tourism and entertainment sectors
  • Investment in renewable energy and cutting-edge technology

Vision for 2030

Mohammed bin Salman's Vision 2030 is a bold blueprint to transform Saudi Arabia into a global investment powerhouse. This ambitious plan aims to:

  • Diversify the economy beyond oil dependency
  • Develop world-class public services in health, education, and infrastructure
  • Foster a vibrant society with rich cultural experiences
  • Create a more open and tolerant social environment
